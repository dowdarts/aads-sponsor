Why We Do This Without Profit

Matthew Dow and Cecil Dow fund this series out of their own pockets — heating costs, DartConnect fees, cameras, and the entire live stream setup. We built this because the players deserve a stage and the community needs a home. We are not selling ads to get rich. We are keeping the sport alive.

Stage Presence — The AADS Difference

Matthew designed AADS after watching talented Atlantic Canadian players underperform at the Canadian Open because they were "stage struck." We provide the lights, the cameras, and the atmosphere — so when our players reach the National or World stage, they've already played like pros.

"By helping us, you are fueling the future. Our goal is to encourage kids to join the sport, bring their friends, and grow the game until Atlantic Canada is the dominant force in World Darts."
